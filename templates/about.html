Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our deepfake detection service

Anton has been trained on a huge dataset and has achieved over 98% accuracy in detecting Deepfake images. However, as deepfake technology evolves, we will continuously update Anton to maintain high accuracy.

Anton can detect various types of image manipulations, including face swaps, facial attribute modifications, and general image manipulation techniques like content-aware fill, clone stamping, and object removal. But General Classification is if Image Deepfake or not.

We prioritize your privacy. All uploaded images are processed on Anton's secure servers and are automatically deleted after analysis is complete. We do not store or use your images for any other purposes.

Currently, Anton focuses on image analysis. For video deepfake detection, you can extract key frames from the video and analyze them individually. We're working on adding video analysis capabilities in the future.
